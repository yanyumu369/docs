| -------- | ------- | -------- | -------- | ---------- | --------- | ---------- | -------- |
| `False`  | `None`  | `True`   | `not`    | `and`      | `or`      | `if`       | `elif`   |
| `else`   | `is`    | `in`     | `for`    | `while`    | `break`   | `continue` | `def`    |
| `lambda` | `pass`  | `return` | `try`    | `except`   | `finally` | `raise`    | `assert` |
| `import` | `from`  | `as`     | `global` | `nonlocal` | `class`   | `del`      | `with`   |
